============================
iDAI GeoServer Documentation
============================


Welcome to iDAI GeoServer Documentation.

iDAI GeoServer uses "geonode", a OpenSource Content Management System (CMS) for geospatial data. It is a web-based application and platform for developing geospatial information systems (GIS) and for deploying spatial data infrastructures (SDI).
